# Pitch Deck Structure

1. **Title:** Momentra, the event media OS for campus communities.
2. **Problem:** Clubs lose photos across phones, drives, chats, and untagged folders.
3. **Audience:** College clubs, societies, fests, student councils, alumni cells, and creator teams.
4. **Product Demo:** Event dashboard, upload queue, masonry gallery, AI search, Find My Photos, QR sharing.
5. **AI Moat:** Smart tags, face search, duplicate detection, moderation, and AI captions.
6. **Cloud Architecture:** Direct S3 uploads, CloudFront delivery, workers, PostgreSQL, Socket.IO.
7. **Access Control:** Admin, Photographer, Club Member, Viewer with public/private albums.
8. **Growth Loops:** QR albums, tagged notifications, watermarked downloads, stories/highlights.
9. **Business Model:** Club SaaS, festival plans, storage tiers, premium AI indexing.
10. **Roadmap:** Mobile app, print store, sponsor analytics, creator marketplace, campus-wide memory graph.
11. **Traction Metrics To Show:** Upload volume, shared albums, active members, AI matches, downloads.
12. **Ask:** Pilot with 10 clubs and two annual fests.
